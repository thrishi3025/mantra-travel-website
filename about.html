Our History

A Vision For Purpose-Driven Exploration

Aetheria Expeditions was founded in 2018 with a simple realization: the most transformative travel experiences happen when we slow down, step off the beaten track, and respect the communities and ecosystems we visit.

What started as a small cooperative of conservationist guides in the Himalayas has grown into an international boutique travel designer. Today, we craft highly customized itineraries across India, ensuring that every expedition is both personally enriching and ecologically regenerative.

We believe in travel that gives back. For every booking made, we dedicate 5% of all profits directly to local conservancy efforts in our host states, working to protect wildlife in the Western Ghats and restore habitats in rural Rajasthan.

Our Commitments

The Foundation of Our Journeys

We do not compromise on our ethics. We believe that premium travel and environmental protection are not mutually exclusive, but rather depend on one another.

Every decision, from the electric vehicles we hire to the ingredients used by our private chefs, is evaluated against strict ecological guidelines. We aim to show that high-end travel can actively heal the regions it traverses.

Carbon Offset Program

We calculate the total carbon footprint of your flights and land transport, offsetting 100% of emissions through verified community-led reforestation programs in Madhya Pradesh.

Direct Community Funding

We source heritage accommodations, utilize local guides, and buy from neighborhood organic farming co-ops to ensure tourism dollars stay inside local economies.

Wildlife Protection

Our excursions strictly adhere to non-disturbance codes. We support local wildlife rehabilitation reserves and avoid any activity that exploits fauna.
